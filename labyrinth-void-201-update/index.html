Labyrinth-Void 2.0.1 Update

June 30th 2022 | By TravsVoid | Blog | Updated on: June 30th 2022

Hi players, a new update has gone live that fixes a few bugs and improves menu navigation. A x86 version is no longer included in the game package since it doubles the file size of the game even though only the x64 package is used on Xbox. I also updated the cover art but to see the change on Xbox you may have to delete and redownload the game.

That's it for this update, if you come across any issues send them my way and I will look into them.
